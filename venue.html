Speech Synthesis Workshop 2027

Speaking in sub-tongues: embracing culture, heritage and diversity in Brazilian accents

Location and Infrastructure

The Speech Synthesis Workshop 2027 and the Blizzard Challenge 2027 will be held at the Centro de Convenções e Eventos Aurélio Buarque de Holanda, located in the Hotel Ritz Lagoa da Anta, a premier venue in Maceió, Alagoas, Brazil. This modern conference center offers state-of-the-art facilities and accessibility features that ensure a seamless experience for all attendees.

Key Venue Features

Large Auditorium for Plenary Sessions

The Pontes de Miranda Hall at the Hotel Ritz Lagoa da Anta accommodates 650 attendees in an auditorium setting, serving as the primary plenary hall.

Dedicated Areas for Additional Activities

Poster Presentation Area: The Foyer (375m²) offers ample space for poster sessions and networking.

Optional Larger Venue

The Centro Cultural e de Exposições Ruth Cardoso offers an auditorium with 1,251 seats, ensuring expanded capacity for keynote lectures and major sessions.

Book Exhibition Space

A dedicated exhibition area will allow publishers and exhibitors to showcase linguistic research materials.

Multiple Parallel Session Rooms

The Centro de Convenções e Eventos Aurélio Buarque de Holanda provides 10+ rooms of different sizes, suitable for workshops, parallel sessions, and special meetings.

Registration and Secretariat Areas

A separate reception ensures smooth participant check-in and logistical support.
