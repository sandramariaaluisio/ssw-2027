Speech Synthesis Workshop 2027

Speaking in sub-tongues: embracing culture, heritage and diversity in Brazilian accents

Call for Sponsorship

We are pleased to invite you to partner with the ISCA Speech Synthesis Workshop (SSW) and Blizzard Challenge 2027, to be held September 6-9, 2027 in Maceió, Alagoas, Brazil.

Hosted in Maceió, a city were the cultural fabric has been woven from Indigenous, African, Portuguese, and diverse immigrant traditions, creating a vibrant setting to examine language interaction, identity construction, and the evolution of communication practices.

Participating in SSW 2027 provides a unique platform to showcase your organisation, engage with key decision-makers and build lasting industry connections. The SSW 2027 Prospectus is now available and offers a range of sponsorship and exhibition opportunities designed to ensure your organisation gains maximum exposure at the congress:

  • Alignment with your Organisational Objectives - Tailored sponsorship packages that align with your strategic goals and enhance your company's visibility.
  • Networking Opportunities - Engage with key decision-makers, industry leaders, and peers to build valuable connections and partnerships.
  • Commitment to the Sector - Demonstrate your organisation's dedication to advancing the field and supporting the professional community.
